# PRODUCT.md — Petron LLC

## Register
brand — the site sells trust and national reach; design IS the marketing.

## Product purpose
A rugged general contractor, fuel-system installer, and commercial real-estate
developer based in Alexandria, Louisiana, "building across the U.S." The website
must (1) prove national reach at a glance, and (2) do real lead-generation work
for the real-estate division.

## Users
- Developers, operators, and corporate real-estate teams vetting a contractor for
  ground-up builds, fuel systems, or government work.
- Prospective commercial tenants searching for office / warehouse / retail space.
- Returning clients (the majority of revenue) who already trust the company.

## Brand & tone
Heavy-duty, plain-spoken, confident. "Building on a firm foundation." The benchmark
is "good experiences with no surprises, always." First-name-basis relationships.
Not boutique, not corporate-slick. Engineered confidence.

## Anti-references
- Dark-luxury-gold real-estate sites (boutique, soft, aspirational).
- Generic SaaS hero-metric templates.
- Full hazard-stripe "construction yellow + black" cliche. Amber is a precise
  functional accent, not wallpaper.

## Strategic principles
- Two flagship features carry the site: the nationwide project map and the
  properties browser. Everything else supports them.
- Every page should feel built, not decorated: structural lines, real data,
  monospaced labels, concrete and steel.
- Proof over adjectives: real clients (Love's, Chevron, Mac's, FireHouse Subs,
  AT&T, P&G, USACE, State of Louisiana) and real portfolio names.
